Innsect creates and extends the concept of bug hotels to address the challenges of industrialization and habitat loss for insects. In response to the growing density of cities and the decline of green spaces, our approach constitutes an adaptable habitat for diverse insect species in highly urbanized environments. We propose a modular system consisting of interconnected cells, resembling to small bug hotels, which can be assembled to form larger colonies throughout the city. By supporting social aspect and promoting engagement with nature, our design aims to cultivate a sense of community involvement and biodiversity conservation.
